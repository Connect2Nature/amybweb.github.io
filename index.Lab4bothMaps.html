Two sides to a story

The 1st map below: Identifies current fish blockages. One can connect these blockages to development very easily. Roads and buildings redirected the water flows. Thus impacting the survival of the fish that spawn in the creaks, streams, and rivers. As well the chain reaction the decline of salmon has on the eco-system that surrounds it.



This had a rippling effect across the landscape and changed the ecolgoy in most of the prairie areas as well. The 2nd map identifies where prairies would have been located. There were many traditional foods that were harvested from these prairies that were also lost. As well as the traditional land managment practices, such as traditional burning.
